What's Your Plan?

Are you planning to attend your favorite concert? We are here to help. We all know it’s a pain in the a** to decide where do you want to eat when you go out. It’s an even worse feeling when you just had a blast at your favorite artist’s concert and afterwards your stomach is just roaring!

We’ve decided to offer YOU, suggestions of the best places to eat around the venue where your event is going to take place. Do not settle for the first food place you see. Some say it’s even worse to go to sleep after a crappy meal rather than an empty stomach, hell, you may even end up sick if you aren’t careful.

Let us help you on your quest to plan your night out.

What Do We Do?

Here are our 3 simple steps.

Find Your Event

Looking for your favorite artist? Say no more. We will show you a list of events that take place at every major venue in the GTA

Make Your Stomach Happy

Don't forget about your stomach! We will give you suggestions for great places to eat around the venue you'll be in.

Sleep Like A King

With a happy stomach and a great memory of attending your favorite artist's concert, you are now allowed to go to sleep.
